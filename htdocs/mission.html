元一品牌
元一能源致力于可持续能源的综合投资服务。团队拥有在互联网、智能硬件、风电、光伏，金融等行业跨界从业经验的优秀人才，从事能源综合投资服务多年，善于综合能源应用的创新设计，通过结合合作伙伴的自身情况，量身设计可持续能源解决方案，帮助合作伙伴摆脱对非可持续能源的依赖，最大程度实现全面可持续能源的使用，以互联网服务进行统一的整合管理，优化供能结构，降低支出，减少碳排放，保护环境。从而推动社会向着全面可持续能源迈进。
Origin Energy engages in providing comprehensive sustainable energy investment services. We have excellent talents of Internet and intelligent hardware across the photovoltaic industry, wind power industry, and financial industry with rich experience. We have been engaged in this sector for many years. We are good at integrating innovative designs in the field of energy application to help partners be independent of non-sustainable energy and make full use of sustainable energy to the greatest extent. By taking the actual situation of partners into consideration, we design specific sustainable energy solutions for them. We conduct unified integration and management by Internet services, optimize the energy supply structure, lower partners’ costs and reduce carbon emission to protect the environment, and hence gradually promote the society towards one of overall clean energy.
元一品牌
能源是人类生存和发展的重要基石，是社会经济运行的动力和基础。 每一次工业革命都离不开能源类型和使用方式的革新，推动着人类社会的发展和进步。目前，随着传统化石能源的逐渐枯竭以及能源消费引起的环境问题日益恶化，未来人类发展与传统能源结构不可持续的矛盾不断尖锐，世界范围内对能源供给与结构转变的需求愈发高涨， 从而催生清洁低碳能源结构与全新能源供给方式的提出。
元一能源的企业使命是从根本上改变传统能源利用模式，推动社会向全面可持续能源应用转变，降低社会用能成本，引导社会对可持续能源和全新能源供给方式的认知，重新构筑新的能源消费和应用格局，改善环境，实现能源的清洁低碳替代和高效可持续发展。
The mission of the Origin Energy company is 1) to fundamentally change the traditional energy use model, 2) to promote the society to become one of overall application of sustainable energy, 3) to reduce social energy costs, 4) to guide the cognition of society on sustainable energy and brand new way of energy supply, 5) to rebuild a new energy consumption and application pattern, 5) to improve the environment, and 6) to achieve clean and low-carbon energy replacement and efficient and sustainable development.
